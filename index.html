What is Vinsters?

Vinsters is about connecting the next generation of wine fans with independent, craft winemakers.

Buy Local, Shop Small. There are hundreds of of local wineries and winemakers who are passionate about their product. Their wines are more natural, more authentic, and they taste better.

Indie wines can be hard to find. Vinsters helps you discover true wines with tours, tastings and events, both in wine country and in your urban back yard.

Vinsters isn't about smelling the bouquets, we believe in just enjoying good wine with good company. You don't have to be an expert to be a Vinster.
